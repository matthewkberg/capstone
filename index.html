Our Space: Queer Community Center

Creating space for the Queer community to gather and create with an abundance mindset.

Problem Statement

The Twin Cities lacks a Queer (LGBTQ+) community space where folks can come together for support, to uplift Queer voices, encourage the art of making and nourish the collective spirit that comes from gathering.
